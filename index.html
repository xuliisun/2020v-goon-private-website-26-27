// 01 - About

Built to Compete.

Team 2020V - Twenty-Twenty Vision is a youth-led, competitive VEX Robotics team aiming to compete at the VEX Robotics World Championship in April 2027. Our team is full of dedicated, relentless, passionate individuals with years of experience in robotics. We're not just building robots - we're learning, innovating, and solving problems under pressure.

Our team combines mechanical design, electronics, and software to field a machine that's fast, reliable, and built to win.

From our first competition scrimmage to qualification events and regionals, our team gives everything a 20/20 effort.

DRIVETRAIN
6-Motor Tank Drive

High-torque 6-motor drivetrain with 4" omni wheels optimized for pushing power and field traversal speed.

INTAKE
Flex Wheel Intake

Rubber flex wheels run at maximum RPM for aggressive game object collection. Zero jams under match pressure.

SCORING
Catapult Launch System

Slip-gear catapult tuned for match load shots with a repeatable flight arc. Minimal charge time between shots.

AUTONOMOUS
PID + Odometry

Closed-loop PID control with odometry tracking for precise positioning. Multiple auton routines selectable pre-match.

PNEUMATICS
Endgame Deployment

Pneumatic-actuated endgame mechanism fires in under 0.3 seconds. Pressure tuned for reliability across repeated activations.

ELECTRONICS
Brain + V5 Smart Motors

Full V5 ecosystem with real-time telemetry monitoring. Rotation sensors on all critical subsystems for data-driven tuning.

STRUCTURE
Aluminium C-Channel Frame

Full aluminium C-channel construction keeps weight low while maintaining rigidity. Every joint double-braced against match impacts.

SENSORS
Inertial + Vision Sensor

IMU-assisted turning for consistent autonomous heading. Vision sensor used for target tracking and game object detection.

NOTEBOOK
Engineering Documentation

Full engineering notebook documenting every design decision, test result, and iteration - built for Excellence Award judging.

STRATEGY
Match Scouting System

Custom scouting spreadsheet tracking opponent cycles, autonomous consistency, and endgame success rates across all events.
